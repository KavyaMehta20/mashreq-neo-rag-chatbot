## ACCOUNT 3: NEO PLUS Saver Account

### Product Overview


| Field              | Details                                                                             |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Exact Account Name | NEO PLUS Saver Account                                                              |
| Account Category   | Savings Account — High-Yield                                                        |
| Currency           | AED only                                                                            |
| Variant Note       | "NEO PLUS Saver Account is a variant of the Easy Saver Account" (from product page) |


**Main Features (from product page and Unified CASA KFS – April 2026):**

- High-yield savings account designed to encourage savings
- Earn 6.25% per annum if salary of AED 10,000+ is transferred to Mashreq NEO Account
- Earn 5% per annum if unable to transfer salary, but maintain at least AED 50,000 monthly average balance
- Interest calculated on monthly average balance; credited monthly
- 2 free debit transactions per month without impacting interest payment
- More than 2 debit transactions in a month = interest for that month forfeited
- Interest earned on balances up to AED 500,000 only (funds above AED 500,000 earn no interest)
- No cheque book, no standing instructions, no auto-debit facility
- No debit card issued with this account
- Cannot be used for international transfers directly
- Cannot be opened as a joint account
- Shariah-compliant option available via Mashreq Al Islami app
- Must be opened through Mashreq Mobile App by existing NEO customers only
- Not available for Gold and Private Banking customers

### Eligibility Requirements

- Must be an existing active NEO customer
- Must hold an active Mashreq NEO Account (Current or Savings)
- "NEO PLUS Saver Account is only available for UAE residents who hold an active Mashreq NEO Account." (from FAQ on product page)
- Not available for Gold and Private Banking customers
- Minimum salary to earn 6.25%: AED 10,000/month transferred to Mashreq NEO Account
- Alternative (5% p.a.): Maintain AED 50,000 monthly average balance
- NEO PLUS Saver status: NEO PLUS tier requires monthly average balance of at least AED 50,000 across all Mashreq accounts OR salary transfer of at least AED 10,000

**Required Documents:**
No documents required. Opened in-app by existing NEO customers.

### Fees and Charges


| Fee Type                    | Amount                  |
| --------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Monthly Minimum Balance Fee | None                    |
| Fall-Below Fee              | None                    |
| Account Opening Fee         | AED 0                   |
| Account Closure             | Via app (no fee stated) |


**Interest Rate Detail (from Unified CASA KFS, April 2026):**

- Interest rates available at: mashreq.com/NPIR
- Effective 1 June 2026, NEO Savings Account basic rate becomes 0%; NEO PLUS Saver rate remains as published on NPIR page

5. **Cross-Border Accounts (Mashreq Egypt Account, Mashreq Pakistan Account)** — these are out of scope for this report.
6. **Interest rates for NEO PLUS Saver Account** are dynamic and published at mashreq.com/NPIR. The current rates referenced (6.25% and 5% p.a.) are taken from the official product page and KFS.
7. **All fees are subject to change** — per KFS: "Service, fees & interest rates may change. For updated fees/charges, you may visit mashreq.com/soc"
8. **Cooling-Off Period** — all accounts carry a 5 business day cooling-off period per UAE Central Bank Consumer Protection Regulation (Circular No. 8 – 2020), as stated in the respective KFS documents.
9. **Credit Card Minimum Income Confirmation** — the noon Credit Card product page does not explicitly state a minimum income in the FAQ, unlike the Cashback and Platinum Plus pages which both state AED 5,000. Based on consistent NEO credit card eligibility standards, AED 5,000 minimum monthly income is the applicable threshold. This should be independently verified via the Schedule of Charges or application process.
